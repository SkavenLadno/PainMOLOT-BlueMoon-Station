drift_handler
/datum/drift_handler

Drives space drift with a smooth move loop (SSnewtonian_movement). Ported from tg/SPLURT-style newtonian drift.

Vars

drift_forceHow much force is behind this drift (used for impulse math and braking)
ignore_next_glideSkip one glide_size echo from the moveloop (postprocess ordering)
old_dirFacing to restore after a drift step (keeps you “sideways” while gliding)
