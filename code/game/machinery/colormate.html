/proc/sanitize_color_preset_keyscolor_presets_* are nested: list(item_type = list(preset_name = value)). The inner keys are user-entered preset names, so clean control characters out of them on load - otherwise a control-character name makes a preset permanently unselectable and undeletable through the UI.
