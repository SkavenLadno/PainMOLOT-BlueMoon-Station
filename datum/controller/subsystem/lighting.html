Procs

scan_stuck_deferred_zlevelsSafety net for the "lighting never loads" report: a z-level left flagged lighting_initialized with orphaned deferred atoms (interrupted on-demand init) is never re-entered by any movement trigger, because update_z only fires on a z CHANGE and a stationary player never re-fires it. This periodic scan finds z-levels that still have parked deferred atoms AND a present occupant (living client or ghost) and re-runs create_lighting_for_zlevel, letting its self-heal guard flush them. Unoccupied deferred z-levels are intentionally left alone (preserving the deferral optimization).
